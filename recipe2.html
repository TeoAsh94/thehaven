Cosmic's Apple Cake
  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 1 cup Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 2 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 4 cups peeled and chopped Tart Apples
  • 1/4 cup chopped Pecans
  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat eggs, sugar, oil, and vanilla.
  2. Combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add to egg mixture and mix well.
  4. Fold in apples and nuts.
  5. Pour into greased 13x9 baking dish.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 55-60 minutes or until toothpick inserted near center comes out clean.
  7. Cool.
  8. This makes a good snacking cake that can be eaten without frosting. If desired, a cream cheese frosting goes well with this cake.
