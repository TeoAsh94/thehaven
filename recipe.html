Cacio e Pepe
  • Salt
  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Ground Pepper
  • Parmesan
  1. Add salt to water and bring to boil
  2. Add spaghetti to boiling water and cook until tender
  3. Drain water from cooked pasta
  4. Add olive oil to pasta and mix evenly
  5. Add ground pepper to taste
  6. Add parmesan and serve
