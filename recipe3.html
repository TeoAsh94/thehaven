Garlic Lemon Spinach
  • Bunch of Spinach
  • Olive Oil or Vegetable Oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  1. Wash Spinach thoroughly and dry with paper towels or a salad spinner.
  2. Heat Oil and Garlic in a coverable pot or pan until Garlic begins browning.
  3. Add Spinach to pot and mix to coat in Oil and Garlic.
  4. Cover the pot/pan for a minute to let Spinach cook.
  5. Uncover and stir for another 30 seconds to a minute.
  6. Remove from heat and mix in a few drops of lemon juice.
  7. Salt to taste and serve
